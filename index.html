Simple by design

Four steps. Zero friction.

No complicated setup. No learning curve. Just open, lock in, and go.

01

Hit Start

One tap to begin your focus session. The timer runs while you work. No categories to pick, no setup friction — just go.

02

Log What You Did

When you stop the timer, a quick text box appears. Type what you worked on — "Solved 3 LC problems" or "Read chapter 7 of OS textbook." Be honest.

03

Get Your Score

An AI agent reads your log, factors in your session length, and gives you a productivity score out of 100. Plus a clean summary of your day.

04

Compete and Grow

See where you stand on friend leaderboards. Check each other's progress. Build streaks together. Accountability is a team sport.
