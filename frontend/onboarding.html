Before We Begin

A quick agreement so we're on the same page — then the real journey starts.

Welcome to LockdIN. By using this platform, you agree to the following terms that govern how we operate, protect your data, and build a community worth being part of.

Your Data, Your Control. We collect only what's necessary to deliver your experience — session times, scores, streak data, and your buddy configuration. We will never sell, share, or exploit your personal information. Your focus data stays yours.

Accountability, Not Surveillance. LockdIN is designed to help you stay consistent through positive reinforcement and friendly competition. We don't monitor what you work on — only how long and how focused you stay. Your work logs are private between you and your scoring engine.

Your Buddy Is Yours. The buddy you choose is a virtual companion that reflects your consistency. It responds to your streaks and effort — not arbitrary metrics. Treat it as your accountability mirror.

Community Standards. When interacting with friends on the leaderboard, you agree to keep things respectful and constructive. We reserve the right to moderate behavior that undermines the positive environment we're building.

Continuous Improvement. LockdIN is evolving. Features, scoring algorithms, and buddy behaviors may change as we improve the platform. We'll always notify you of meaningful updates that affect your experience.

By proceeding, you confirm that you've read and agree to these terms. You can review them anytime from your profile settings.
